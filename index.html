What did I learn?

Each event in a story happens in a special order so it makes sense and feels exciting to read! The order of events is the way things happen in a story, one step at a time.

Here’s a simple way to remember why the order of events in a story is important:

  • It Makes the Story Clear: When events are in the right order, it’s easy to follow and understand what is happening.
  • It Keeps the Story Exciting: The order helps build the story step by step, making it fun to read or listen to.
  • It Solves Problems Step by Step: Stories show how problems are solved, one event at a time like Felix helping Twinkle get back to the sky.
  • It Helps Us Remember: When events are in the right order, we can retell the story more easily.
